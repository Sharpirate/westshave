History

Jack Smith at West Shave, 1970

West Shave was founded in 1967 by Jack Smith, a professional barber who had just moved to Louisiana and wanted to open his own barbershop.

Shortly after its opening, the company became a huge success with dozens of clients everyday. The popularity of the company continued to grow steadily over the years and now it's one of the most well-known barbershops in Louisiana.

Our Team

Our team consists of four professional barbers with a great attention to detail who are really passionate about their craft. Two of them are no other but Jack's grandsons.

We believe in making men look their best. Remember… it’s not just a haircut, it’s an experience.
